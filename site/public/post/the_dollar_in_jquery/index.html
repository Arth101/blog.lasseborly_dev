Ok, I have been using jQuery for the last two years and even had a lecturer (Anne-Gaelle Colom) which is one of the few team members of jQuery.What I’m about to tell you I did not know. I have used jQuery in periods on a daily basis for almost two years and this never occurred to me.The $ in jQuery is the same as writing jQuery.Instead of writing jQuery.isNumeric('420');You can use the shorthand way and write $.isNumeric('420');.I thank Martin Graversen for the realization and wonder why I never caught onto that myself.
